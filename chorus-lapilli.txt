Modified the click handling logic in the Board component to begin implementing chorus lapilli rules. I added logic to count how many pieces the current player has on the board and restricted piece placement so that each player can only place up to three pieces.
After a player has three pieces, additional clicks on empty squares no longer place new pieces. This prepares the game for the next phase, where players will move existing pieces instead of placing new ones.
