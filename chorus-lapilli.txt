Implemented the basic movement phase for chorus lapilli. After a player has placed three pieces, the game switches from placing new pieces to moving existing ones.
I implemented a two-click system:
- The first click selects one of the current player's pieces
- The second click selects a destination square
If the destination square is empty, the selected piece is moved there. After the move, the selection resets.
I tested the behavior in the browser and confirmed that:
- Players can no longer place new pieces after reaching three pieces
- Players can select and move their pieces using two clicks
- The game still alternates turns correctly
